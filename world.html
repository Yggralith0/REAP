Earth

Due to Yaldabaoth's power of creation, various life forms have sprung up. REAP wishes to forge ties with the new xeno creatures. However, there are now beasts known as "Devas" that roam Earth and though they tend to be calm around Humans, there are those that will attack on sight.

Affinity World

The Affinity World is a chart that shows the ties people have with one another. How they feel about a certain person is shown in the Affinity World. It is great to build affinity with those around you.
