About REAP

REAP stands for the Recovery of Earth After the Pillage. REAP is a top secret military organization formed to take Earth back from Yaldabaoth and restore Earth back to its former self. It was formed by the man behind Yaldabaoth. Members of REAP are known as 'Reapers'. To know more about REAP, how bout experiencing it yourself? REAP is always open to those eager to reclaim Earth back.
