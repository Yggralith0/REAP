Classes

In REAP you will get to choose from a total of 8 classes, 1 being hidden in the game. Do not worry, you can change your classes whenever you like, so choose the class that fits your style! Now here are the classes1

Neophyte

Description: You start off in this class, basically, it's the beginners class. Don't think it's easy to master this class though!
Weapons: Dagger, Dual Handguns
Talent Slots: 3

Rambo

Description: Wanna be the life of the party? Then the Rambo is the class for you! Go into battle and deliver the heavy blows! GET WILD!!
Weapons: ShoulderCannon, QuakeBall
Talent Slots: 3

Protector

Description: Protectors are the main healers. They stay far from the scene of battle, giving support to their comrades. Don't worry they can fight too!
Weapons: Sniper Rifle, Shield
Talent Slots: 3

Gamer

Description: Once a Gamer, always a Gamer is what they say. This class is for the combo lovers. Rack up the damage and get the high score!
Weapons: DualBlades, RapidGun
Talent Slots: 3

Deadly Berserker

Description: A promotion after the Rambo class. Inject fear into your enemies with that psychotic smile! Nothing will stop you! Not even your rather low defense.
Weapons: ShoulderCannon, HeavyKnuckles
Talent Slots: 4

Analytic Guardian

Description: A promotion after the Protector class. Protect your allies from danger and cause status effects like Paralysis and Bug to your enemies!
Weapons: Sniper Rifle, Shield
Talent Slots: 4

Galactic Crusader

Description: A promotion after the Gamer class. Deliver the damage swiftly. Think you can keep up?
Weapons: DualBlades, RapidGun
Talent Slots: 4

CLASSIFIED

Mastermind

Description: Only the best of the best can hold this class. Think you got what it takes to be in the class with high offensive, defensive and evasive attacks as you protect your party and analyse the battle for the perfect approach?Then let us see if you have what it takes.
Weapons: Psycho Launchers, Winged Saber
Talent Slots: 5
